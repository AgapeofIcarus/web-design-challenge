Weather vs Distance to the Equator: A Summary

For this project, I utilized APIs to gather data regarding weather. With this data, I used Matplotlib to generate visualisations to display the differences caused by distance to the equator. The purpose of this site is to display my findings in an easy to view manner.


Not only images will be supplied through these pages. Summaries, analysis, and the general findings of the project are here for you to explore. These findings were based on factors such as: latitude, longitude, humidity, wind speed, and cloudiness. Come and explore the data with me.
